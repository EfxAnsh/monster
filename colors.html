The Color Wheel

Organizing the spectrum for maximum visual impact.

The color wheel is a circular diagram that organizes colors and shows relationships between them.

Primary Colors

The foundation of all hues. These cannot be created by mixing other colors.

Red
Blue
Yellow

Secondary Colors

Created by mixing primary colors. These add depth to any design.

Green

Color Schemes

Analogous

Neighbors on the wheel. Smooth and harmonious transitions.

Complementary

Opposites on the wheel. High energy and extreme contrast.

Triadic

Perfectly spaced triangle. Balanced, vibrant, and diverse.

Monochromatic

One hue, different depths. Clean, modern, and sophisticated.

Design Identification

Selected Scheme: Complementary

My Monster Energy design utilizes a Complementary color scheme. By pairing high-vibrancy Monster Green against a Deep Black background with Red accents, I create maximum visual tension.

Missing: An Analogous scheme. My design avoids "neighbors" on the wheel to ensure every element stands out aggressively.
